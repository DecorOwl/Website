What we do

Buying home decor products from local vendors through online platform has been restrictive due to certain barriers and, this industry has not been benifitted from the digitization of commerical commodity(e-commerce). DecorOwl aims to break those barrier through the use of 21st century promising technology - Agumented Reality (AR) and Artificial Intelligence (AI).

DecorOwl is an E-Marketplace which connects local home decor vendors to a single platform. DecorOwl provides smart AI solution to vendors to increase their customer outreach, and AR-AI based solution to customer to smartly select product of their need.
